Welcome to iKnow IMERI FKUI
A medical-based knowledge center and digital library developed by IMERI FKUI. This media preserves and enables easy and open access to all types of digital literacy content, such as publication journal articles, case reports, books, and theses that are produced or affiliated with the academic community within the Faculty of Medicine, Universitas Indonesia.
